After you have entered your name and clicked ahead, you can either join a game or create one. To create, you need to enter a name (of the game... ofcourse). The time for each play (time for each alphabet is a play) is selected to 30 seconds, that can be changed. The alphabets you want to play for is selected to the complete english alphabet (!) or you can change the number of alphabets and also, you can choose the alphabets you want to play for Watch:
   To join a game, select a game from the list.

   After creation or joining a game, the game are will show up, that will have a list of players in the game (to the left). Four fields for the name, place, animal thingy where you need to type quick and correct to get points. A chat (to the right or click ) where you can write to the players in the game. After you submit youll have to wait for others to finish the play. The play will also end automatically, if the time runs out. Watch:
